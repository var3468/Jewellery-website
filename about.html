About ShineAura

ShineAura is a boutique jewellery brand focused on minimal, everyday luxury.

All designs are handcrafted in small batches using recycled gold, sterling silver and conflict-free stones.

Each piece is quality checked to ensure comfort, durability and long-lasting shine.
